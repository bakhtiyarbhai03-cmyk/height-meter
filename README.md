Tips for Maximum Accuracy
Pivot, Don't Lift: When moving the camera from the target's feet to their head, just tilt your wrists. Do not raise or lower your arms. The trigonometry math assumes the phone stays at the exact same point in space for both angle captures.
Calibrate the Horizon: If you notice the angles seem a bit jumpy, hold your phone perfectly straight (so you are looking flat at the horizon) and tap the Calibrate 0° button in the top right corner.
Distance is Key: Because the underlying formula is H = d \cdot (\tan(\theta_{head}) - \tan(\theta_{feet})), any error in the distance (d) you enter will directly multiply the error in the final height. If you guess the distance, the height will also be a guess!
